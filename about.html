About Me

I am a recent PhD graduate at the University of Southern California, where I worked under Prof. Kristina Lerman on social networks. My research focuses on Recommender Systems applied to Online Social Networks, exploring how these systems mediate our connections and influence our online experiences.

Background

I have a diverse academic background: in computer science, I have experience in distributed computing, natural language processing, and machine learning. In biology I am experienced with cellular and microbiology, and microbial ecology. My current work spans multiple disciplines, leveraging these skills to address key challenges in computational social science.
